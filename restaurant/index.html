The Victorian is a charismatic eatery and drinkery to indulge in mouthwatering meat classics, crunchy house-made chips and creative salads. Antique paintings adorn the walls and its large windows offer a delightful view of the surrounding nature. Established in 2010 by a young couple of chefs with a vision to offer gourmet-quality classics at an affordable price along natural scenery.
